Background

Organization background

PT. Dafema Trans Logistics is an international freight forwarder, customs broker, and logistics service provider that has served a wide range of industries in Indonesia and abroad. We understand that client trust is built through consistency, reliability, and clarity in every shipment.

With a network of partners across major ports and airports, we connect our clients' logistics needs to efficient solutions — for both routine shipments and large-scale project cargo.
