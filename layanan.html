What we do

Our services

End to end logistics solutions | from document handling to final delivery.

Air freight

Air shipments for cargo that needs speed and reliable timing, backed by partners at major airports.

  • Air cargo consolidation
  • Door-to-door delivery
  • Handling for time-sensitive, special cargo

Sea freight

FCL (Full Container Load) and LCL (Less than Container Load) services for domestic and international sea shipments.

  • FCL & LCL shipping
  • Vessel space booking
  • Shipment status tracking

Land transport

Land distribution to reach your cargo's final destination anywhere in Indonesia.

  • Intercity trucking
  • Last-mile distribution
  • Project cargo delivery

Customs brokerage

Careful documentation and customs handling so your cargo moves smoothly through ports and airports.

  • Import & export documentation
  • Customs regulation consulting
  • Coordination with customs authorities

Warehousing

Storage and warehouse management to keep your supply chain running smoothly before final distribution.

  • Short- & long-term storage
  • Inventory management
  • Cross-docking

Project cargo

Specialized planning for heavy, oversized, or non-standard project cargo.

  • Route survey & planning
  • Heavy equipment handling
  • Multimodal coordination
